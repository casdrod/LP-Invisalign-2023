How Invisalign Works

Starting with a digital model of your mouth, we are able to utilize the latest technology to fabricate a series of clear aligners. Used in sequence, these thin, plastic devices move your teeth into their ideal positions – quickly and painlessly. Your entire treatment is based on a computer simulation of your “perfect smile”, and we can achieve amazing results comfortably and predictably. Invisalign is an ideal solution for teens and adults with routine orthodontic challenges, and everyone benefits from fewer trips to the dentist. Most importantly, fewer visits means time and money savings for you and your family.
